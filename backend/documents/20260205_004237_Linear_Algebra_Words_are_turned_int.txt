Linear Algebra: Words are turned into numerical vectors (embeddings) and matrices, allowing for semantic understanding through operations like dot products and matrix multiplication (e.g., in self-attention).

Calculus: Derivatives (gradient descent) are crucial for training models, as they help adjust parameters to minimize errors.

Probability & Statistics: Conditional probability helps predict the next word, while concepts like mean, variance, and standard deviation are fundamental.
